COLOUR RUN 2017



On 30 August 2017, my friends and I participated in the Color Run held in Johor Bahru.

A total of 10 thousand participants participated, namely school students, civil servants, residents and all walks of life.

The run was a lot of fun as there were a variety of exciting activities.

The run is 10km for men and 8km for women.

Various attractive prizes are provided such as certificates of participation, food, drinks, medals, vouchers and stationery.

The fee is RM50 for each participant.
